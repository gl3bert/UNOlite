Welcome to UNOlite, a simplified version of a popular game of UNO.

This game uses cards from 1 to 7, S (Skip), +1 (Draw One), one each of four colors; it also has  W (Wild), and +2 (Draw Two) cards, which the player selects colors of. The gameplay is essentially the same as the regular UNO - match colors and powers, draw a card when no matching cards are on hand, play Wild/Draw Two cards whenever. This game does not allow "stacking," placing two cards at once or reversing Draw cards. To win, the player must get to zero cards before their opponent. Your opponent is smartly written code. If the game comes to the point when no side is able to play a card, click "Stuck?" button.

Have fun!
